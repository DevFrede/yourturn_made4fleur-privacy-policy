Privacy Policy for YourTurn by Made4Fleur
Last updated: April 2026

Introduction
============
This Privacy Policy explains how the application (“the App”) handles user data.
This app is built in good faith to respect your privacy and provide a secure, offline experience. The App operates entirely on the user’s device and does not connect to the internet or transmit any information externally.

Data Collection
===============
The App does not collect personal data in the sense of transmitting, storing, or processing information outside the user’s device.

However, the App may store information locally on the device as part of its normal functionality.
This locally stored data is used only within the App to display status, preferences, or user‑entered information.

No data is ever sent to the developer or to any third party.

Local Data Storage
==================
All information created or entered in the App is stored exclusively on the user’s device.
This includes any activity, settings, or content the user generates.
- The data remains private to the user
- The App does not upload or sync data
- The App does not use cloud services
- The App does not use external servers
  
If the user deletes the App, all locally stored data is removed automatically by the operating system.

Data Sharing
============
The App does not share any data with:
- Third parties
- Advertisers
- Analytics providers
- External servers
- Social networks

No information ever leaves the device.

Internet Usage
==============
The App does not use the internet.
It does not send or receive any network requests.

Children’s Privacy
==================
Because the App does not collect or transmit any data, it is safe for users of all ages.
No personal information is collected from children.

Changes to This Policy
======================
If the App’s functionality changes in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.
Users are encouraged to review the policy periodically.
